Our Services

Testing Services

NICS has an independent laboratory providing consulting, analysis, field & laboratory testing for structural reliability and in-service quality.

We offer a wide variety of testing services from one-time to daily production testing services. Working with oil and gas industry and other corporate organization our testing services are tailored to meet your quality control and assurance needs

Conventional Nondestructive Testing

  • Pressure Testing
  • Post Weld Heat Treatment (PWHT)
  • Ultrasonic Testing (UT)
  • Dye Penetrant Testing (DPT)
  • Magnetic Particle Inspection (MPI)
  • Radiographic Testing (RT)
  • Visual Testing (VT)
  • Vacuum Box Testing
  • Hardness Testing
  • Flow Rate Measurement
  • Holiday Testing
  • Coating Thickness measurement.

Advanced Non-Destructive Testing

  • Computed Radiography Testing (CRT)
  • Pulsed Eddy current Inspection (PEC)
  • Long range ultrasonic testing (LRUT)
  • Corrosion Mapping
  • Phased Array Ultrasonic Testing (PAUT)
  • Digital Radiographic Testing (DRT)
  • Time of Flight Diffraction (TOFD)
  • Non-contact Magnetometry
  • Destructive Testing
