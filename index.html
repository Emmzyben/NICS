Corporate
Overview

NICS Inspection Consultancy Services Limited (NICS) is a fast growing testing and inspection services company offering a broad range of professional services to the oil and gas industry and other corporate organizations in Nigeria; NICS plans, manages and delivers fully integrated testing and inspection solution on both land and sea.

NICS works alongside leading industry operators and takes responsibility for client projects. The result is evident in our portfolio and lasting relationships with our clients.
